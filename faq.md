# FAQ

Here are the most frequently asked questions about OpsFlow.

---

## ❓ Q1: Is OpsFlow free?
Yes, OpsFlow is open-source and completely free to use.

---

## ❓ Q2: Can I deploy OpsFlow on Kubernetes?
Absolutely! OpsFlow supports Kubernetes deployment.  
We even provide Helm charts for easy setup.

---

## ❓ Q3: Where can I report bugs?
Please open a GitHub issue in our official repository:  
[https://github.com/<username>/<repo-name>/issues](https://github.com/<username>/<repo-name>/issues)

---

## ❓ Q4: Does OpsFlow support custom domains?
Yes. You can configure your GitHub Pages deployment to work with a custom domain like `docs.opsflow.in`.

---

## ❓ Q5: How do I get help?
- Email us: **support@opsflow.in**
- Join our community forum (coming soon!)
